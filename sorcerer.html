Portfolio Thread > Egyptian Sorcerer Sculpture

    Egyptian Sorcerer final release

  • Welcome to my Egyptian Sorcerer Controller work-in-progress development page. Some views on the project, this is the other half for my dragon project final; Less time was spent here on this part of the project, as it was quite interesting week for me and I admit few areas could be tweak but love the result so far.


    • Final asset development

    Final details work done to the robe, hammer, and armor. The hammer show strength and agility, his robe was a challenge, and the positioning of it, implementing the necessary wrinkles and folds in the cloth was somewhat frustrating. There are still room for improvement few areas I have notice could be worked on a bit more, but the overall all result was worth the effort hope you like. Thanks.

  • Final upper body details

    Ok! Here is the final work done on his face with some Egyptian mark encryption and chest detailing as well, his is position to be holding up his robe and with focus work done to his hands.

  • Working on the overall anatomy

    In this part of the project I am focusing on the muscular system a bit more, bringing them out mostly in the chest and arm areas. Normally there are muscles in the human body that are rarely seen, if you are not as fit, I guess! I try implemented these areas in the mesh, hope you like. Take care.
