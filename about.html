About Me Thread



about.me

Jefferson Nah is a Technical Artist with 5 years’ work experience as 3D Technical Artist. He has experience in scripting and programming in web development and animation. Past duties are characters rigging with Mel and Python scripts, 3d modeling/sculpting, texturing environments and characters. He also has experience and expertise in website development and has built few cliental business website using languages HTML5/CSS/Flash/Action Script 3.0/Javascript/JQuery/PHP

Mr. Nah attended Full Sail University in winter park, FL and graduate September of 2005 with an Associate of Science in Computer Animation and currently at Regis University in Denver Colorado where he study four years of Computer Science. Early in his career Jefferson worked for Digital Embryo as a 3d environment artist on summer sport Paradise Island for the Nintendo Wii.
