    Sorcerer Controller and his Dragon, release final

    7/19/2010,. The final release combine both the dragon and his master sorcerer including all the asset and positioning them together, the story behind this piece is pretty interesting as it reflects early Egyptians history of a “Sorcerer Controller and his Dragon” . The over view process for my Sorcerer Controller and his Dragon from concepts to clay marquette sculpting to the early blocking stages, below are some of the creative curves that went into this project. Thanks for viewing.

    • Adding details to the upper body and overall mesh

    The focus at this point of the project is adding large and small details. Folds in the skin as well as blending with areas with less wrinkles, and making sure that certain areas in the skin has a good amount of folds mostly in the neck and chest area. And making sure that the overall anatomy is still consistent in the mesh. Now there is a good amount of detail here for my dragon I amm satisfy with this version of the model.

  • Progress on the wing

    Work-in-progress In Maya, controlling the geometry is very important at the early stages, and the necessary areas that have bending or folding has to be added via edge looping to the blocking stages of the model very important if considering for the model animated. Take care.

  • Dragon maquette reference

    Reference guide, design with Super Sculpey. Soft and pliable until you bake it then you have the option to carved, sanded and drilled when finish baking. Take care.
